Certification framework

Tiered trust levels for healthcare AI voice systems.

The NHID-Clinical Certification Framework provides three progressive trust tiers for AI voice systems operating in B2B healthcare administrative workflows. Certification is voluntary and additive: L2 requires L1, and L3 requires L2.

L1 Baseline

Minimum conformance

The system passes all five Conformance Test Suite tests under controlled evaluation conditions. This is the entry point for claiming NHID-Clinical alignment.

  • All five CTS tests passed.
  • Internal QA team self-attestation.
  • Signed declaration and retained evidence.
  • Tier 1 structured logs present and verifiable.
L2 Operational

Production-ready evidence

The system maintains CTS conformance in production, with structured evidence of ongoing operational compliance and review readiness.

  • All L1 requirements.
  • Minimum 30 days of production audit logs.
  • Disclosure Failure Rate under 2%.
  • Evidence package review by NHID-Clinical maintainer.
L3 Enterprise

Independent verification

Full independent verification of CTS conformance and operational evidence by a qualified third-party auditor.

  • All L1 and L2 requirements.
  • Third-party auditor evaluation.
  • Tier 3 analytics reporting.
  • Annual renewal and registry listing when available.

How to claim certification

L1 process

Run all five CTS tests internally, document results, sign and retain the L1 declaration statement, then add the L1 badge to product documentation. Organizations may notify the NHID-Clinical maintainer at validation@nhid-clinical.org for community acknowledgement.

L2 process

Complete L1, operate in production for at least 30 days, collect Tier 1 and Tier 2 evidence, and submit an evidence summary for informal review before adding the L2 badge.

L3 process

Complete L1 and L2, engage a qualified third-party auditor, submit the full evidence package including auditor attestation, and work with the maintainer on formal review and registry listing as the v1.4 roadmap matures.
