Governance Simulator

Operational prototype for administrative healthcare voice AI — payer–provider workflows, disclosure timing, escalation handling, and logging visibility.

US payer–provider administrative workflows. Not an EU AI Act implementation.

How to use this simulator

  1. Select a scenario from the dropdown on the left
  2. Check the behaviors that were observed during the call
  3. Click Generate Review to produce an operational review
  4. Read the event timeline and workflow notes on the right
