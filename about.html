About NHID-Clinical

An open proposal for transparent AI voice agents in healthcare.

A voluntary set of behavioral guidelines — not an official standard, certification program, or regulatory requirement.

Current Status (May 2026): This is an early open proposal, not an official standard or certification program. Actively seeking feedback and real-world testing from people building or using healthcare voice AI.

Who Created This?

Brianna Baynard started this proposal after seeing real operational problems in healthcare payer–provider phone calls. Too many AI systems hide that they are automated, leading to wasted time, trust issues, and unnecessary call terminations.

What Is NHID-Clinical?

It is a simple, voluntary set of behavioral guidelines for AI voice agents making administrative calls (claims status, eligibility, prior authorization, etc.).

The core idea is straightforward: AI systems should clearly identify themselves as non-human before exchanging operational data.

Core Goals

  • Reduce "impersonation latency" — the delay and friction when humans can't tell they're talking to automation
  • Eliminate deceptive audio tricks (fake breathing, typing sounds, etc.)
  • Provide clear escalation to a real person when needed
  • Create basic audit trails for accountability
  • Address the authorization gap (see NHID-Auth companion proposal)

Scope

In scope

  • Provider-to-payer administrative calls
  • Claims, eligibility, prior authorization
  • Calls involving NPI, member ID, claim numbers

Out of scope

  • Patient-facing calls
  • Clinical advice systems
  • General IAM frameworks

How You Can Help

The problem

Impersonation Latency

AI voice agents are calling payer lines on behalf of providers — requesting claim status, eligibility details, and prior authorization. The agents give NPI numbers and member IDs to authenticate. Payer reps, following their script, provide the information. 3–5 minutes later, something feels off.

The rep asks: "Are you a real person?" The AI admits it's automated. The call is terminated. Protected operational data was already shared. No audit trail. The AI calls back 10 minutes later. Same cycle.

AI voice call enters the workflow
Rep provides NPI, member ID, and workflow details over 3–5 minutes
"Wait — are you an AI?"
Call terminated. Data already shared. No audit trail.
NHID-Clinical: disclosure before any data exchange
