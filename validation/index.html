Why validate?

What validation gives you

Give payer operations teams a standard they can accept. Certified AI callers bypass "are you human?" challenge cycles.

Process

How validation works

1

Submit request

Fill out the validation request form with your company name, contact, target tier, and a brief description of your AI voice agent system.

2

Receive CTS

Within 3 business days, we send you the Conformance Test Suite and evidence requirements for your target tier.

3

Run tests

Execute the five CTS tests (IDG-01 through ATR-01) against your system. Compile your evidence package per tier requirements.

4

Submit evidence

Send your evidence package for review. We confirm pass/fail on each test and issue your certification.

5

Receive badge

On confirmation, receive your L1/L2/L3 certification badge for use in documentation and payer agreements.

→

Timeline

L1: typically 1–2 weeks. L2/L3 depends on evidence collection and review complexity.

Validation tiers

Choose your target tier

L1

Baseline

Self-attestation. Internal QA. Best starting point for most organizations.

  • Signed declaration
  • Script documentation
  • Call logs with timestamps
L2

Operational

30+ days production evidence. DFR < 2%. Evidence review by NHID-Clinical.

  • Production call records
  • DFR calculation
  • Evidence package review
L3

Enterprise

Independent auditor. Continuous monitoring. Contact us before applying.

  • Third-party audit
  • Continuous monitoring
  • Annual renewal
