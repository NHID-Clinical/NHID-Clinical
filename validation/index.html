Why validate?

NHID-Clinical establishes an operational standard for AI voice agent identity disclosure in B2B healthcare workflows. Validation demonstrates a commitment to transparent deployment and gives payer procurement teams a common vocabulary for evaluating AI voice vendors.

Differentiate

Show that your product has considered disclosure, escalation, and audit readiness.

Build trust

Give payers and providers a clearer basis for accepting compliant AI calls.

Align early

Prepare for emerging expectations around AI disclosure in healthcare operations.

How validation works

The pilot process is intentionally simple: submit a request, receive the NHID-Clinical Conformance Test Suite, execute the tests, submit evidence, and receive a validation badge or feedback on remaining gaps.

L1 Baseline

Core disclosure controls

  • Identity disclosure before data request.
  • Organization and purpose identification.
  • Basic escalation path.
L2 Operational

Production audit readiness

  • All L1 controls.
  • Pre-data exchange gate enforcement.
  • Audit log timestamps and operational evidence.
L3 Enterprise

Advanced controls

  • All L2 controls.
  • Bot-to-bot deadlock handling.
  • IVR resilience and identity-layer readiness.
