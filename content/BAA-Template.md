\## Purpose



This Business Associate Agreement governs the use of NHID-Clinical hosted API services 

in connection with workflows that may involve Protected Health Information (PHI) as 

defined under HIPAA (45 CFR §160.103).



\---



\## NHID-Clinical Commitments



\*\*We never store PHI, call audio, or member data.\*\*  

NHID-Clinical processes only:

\- Anonymized trace events (timestamps, policy decisions)

\- Authorization attestations (NPI, scope, duration)

\- Audit log metadata (session IDs, event types)



\*\*Security Posture:\*\*

\- All data processed in HIPAA-aligned environments

\- SOC 2 Type I completed — Type II audit scheduled Q3 2026

\- mTLS and client-certificate authentication available on Enterprise tier

\- Request signing available for all API responses

\- Full audit log export in HL7 FHIR AuditEvent bundle or CSV on demand

\- Breach notification within 24 hours per HIPAA requirements

\- Data retention: audit traces retained for 6 years per HIPAA minimum



\*\*Subcontractors:\*\*  

NHID-Clinical will execute BAAs with any subcontractors who may access PHI on our behalf.



\---



\## Permitted Uses



NHID-Clinical may use and disclose PHI only to:

1\. Perform services described in the underlying service agreement

2\. Comply with applicable law

3\. Respond to data subject requests where required



\---



\## Prohibited Uses



NHID-Clinical will not:

\- Use or disclose PHI for any purpose not permitted by this agreement

\- Sell PHI to third parties

\- Use PHI for marketing without explicit authorization



\---



\## Your Responsibilities



As a Covered Entity, you agree to:

\- Notify NHID-Clinical of any restrictions on PHI use that affect our services

\- Not request NHID-Clinical to use PHI in a manner that would violate HIPAA

\- Report any known security incidents involving NHID-Clinical systems



\---



\## Term and Termination



This agreement remains in effect for the duration of the service relationship.  

Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.  

Upon termination, NHID-Clinical will return or destroy all PHI within 30 days.



\---



\## Liability



NHID-Clinical assumes unlimited liability for breaches resulting from willful neglect.
