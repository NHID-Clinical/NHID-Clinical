Release May 2026

NHID-Clinical v1.3 Released

v1.3 defines five proposed requirements (IDG-01 through ATR-01) for transparent AI voice agent behavior in healthcare payer–provider calls. Includes NHID-Auth v1.0 companion proposal addressing the authorization gap.

Open Proposal May 2026

Early Testing Open

Real-world testing and feedback on the five proposed requirements is now open. If you work in healthcare voice AI — as a payer, provider, or platform — your experience will directly improve the next version.

Companion Spec May 2026

NHID-Auth v1.0 Published

NHID-Auth v1.0 is the companion proposal addressing the authorization gap — defining how an AI agent proves it is authorized to act on behalf of a provider, not just who it claims to be. Independent of NHID-Clinical; organizations may implement it alongside or separately.
