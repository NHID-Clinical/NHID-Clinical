NHID-Clinical v1.3 Released

v1.3 introduces the full Conformance Test Suite (5 deterministic tests), L1/L2/L3 certification tiers, FHIR technical mapping, registry architecture, NHID-Auth v1.0 companion specification, and document layer structure separating normative from informative content.

Companion Spec May 2026

NHID-Auth v1.0 Published

NHID-Auth v1.0 is the companion specification addressing the authorization gap — defining how an AI agent proves it's authorized to act on behalf of a provider, not just who it claims to be. NHID-Auth is independent of NHID-Clinical conformance; organizations may implement it alongside or separately.
