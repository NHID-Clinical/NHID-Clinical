Optional Reference Material

For Payers & Procurement Teams

Considering AI voice agents in your payer–provider workflows? You may find the NHID-Clinical proposed requirements useful as optional reference material when evaluating or contracting with vendors.

Clear Disclaimer: This is not a formal standard, mandatory requirement, or accredited framework. It is one individual's open proposal you can choose to reference if it fits your needs.

Suggested RFP Language (Optional / Illustrative)

The following is example language only. Adapt it to your needs, or ignore it entirely.

Vendors operating AI voice agents are encouraged to consider
alignment with the NHID-Clinical v1.3 open proposal, including:

• Clear upfront disclosure that the caller is an automated /
  non-human system before any operational data exchange

• No deceptive audio artifacts intended to mimic human behavior

• A clear escalation path to a human representative when requested

• Basic audit logging of disclosure timing

Vendors may optionally request informal peer feedback on their implementation at validation@nhid-clinical.org.

Why Some Payers Might Reference This

  • Reduces staff time lost to repeated "are you human?" challenges
  • Shortens the window where operational data is shared with undisclosed systems
  • Provides clearer expectations in vendor discussions
