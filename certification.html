Certification Overview

How NHID-Clinical Certification Works

All certification levels require passing all five conformance tests (IDG-01, PDX-01, DBC-01, EIT-01, ATR-01). Levels differ in evidence depth, verification method, and cost. Choose the level that matches your operational readiness and compliance requirements.

Core Principle: All levels require the same five tests to pass. The difference is in how you demonstrate compliance and how deeply we validate your production environment.

Three Certification Levels

L1, L2, and L3 Explained

L1

Baseline (Self-Attestation)

Cost: Free

Your organization signs a compliance declaration confirming that your AI voice agent passes all five NHID-Clinical tests. You provide internal QA documentation and test results.

What You Get:

  • NHID-Clinical L1 badge for your website
  • Listing in public registry
  • Permission to reference compliance in RFPs
  • Access to community resources

Timeline:

Typically 5–7 business days from submission

Best For:

Early-stage vendors, internal deployments, or organizations seeking baseline compliance without third-party validation.

L2

Operational (Production Validation)

Cost: $500

We validate your system against live production data. You provide 30+ days of call logs, transcripts, and audit trails. We independently verify that all five tests pass in your actual operational environment.

What You Get:

  • NHID-Clinical L2 badge (higher trust signal)
  • Validation report with test results
  • Enhanced registry listing
  • Case study eligibility
  • Direct support for RFP responses

Timeline:

30–45 days (includes 30-day observation period)

Best For:

Vendors seeking payer trust, RFP competitiveness, or regulatory readiness. Recommended for production deployments.

L3

Enterprise (Third-Party Audit)

Cost: Custom (typically $2,500–$5,000)

Full third-party security audit of your voice AI system, including architecture review, code inspection, and compliance verification. Independent auditor validates all five tests and produces a detailed audit report.

What You Get:

  • NHID-Clinical L3 badge (highest trust)
  • Third-party audit report
  • Premium registry listing with audit badge
  • Marketing rights and case study
  • Annual re-audit option
  • Direct engagement with NHID-Clinical governance

Timeline:

60–90 days (includes architecture review, testing, and reporting)

Best For:

Enterprise vendors, regulated entities, or organizations requiring independent security validation for compliance or procurement.

Certification Timeline

From Submission to Badge

L1: 5–7 Days

  1. Submit self-attestation form
  2. Provide QA documentation
  3. Review and approval
  4. Badge issued

L2: 30–45 Days

  1. Submit production data (30 days)
  2. NHID-Clinical validation (10–15 days)
  3. Report generation
  4. Badge and registry listing

L3: 60–90 Days

  1. Audit engagement and scoping
  2. Architecture and code review
  3. Production validation
  4. Audit report and badge

Getting Started

How to Apply for Certification

Step 1: Review the Specification

Read the full NHID-Clinical v1.3 specification and ensure your system passes all five conformance tests.

Step 2: Choose Your Level

Decide whether L1 (free), L2 ($500), or L3 (custom) best fits your needs.

Step 3: Submit Application

Email help@nhid-clinical.org with:

  • Your organization name and contact info
  • Desired certification level
  • Brief description of your voice AI system
  • For L2/L3: production data or audit scope

Step 4: Validation & Badge

We'll validate your submission and issue your badge within the timeline for your chosen level.

Transparency Commitment

L1 Free Forever

The NHID-Clinical specification and L1 certification will always be free and open-source. We believe baseline compliance should not be a financial barrier to adoption. Revenue from L2 and L3 certification services funds ongoing maintenance and governance.

If you have questions about certification or need guidance on which level is right for your organization, reach out to help@nhid-clinical.org.
