Certification framework

Three tiers. Progressive trust.

L1 through L3 define escalating levels of evidence, oversight, and auditability. All tiers require passing all five CTS tests. Tiers differ in evidence depth and verification method.

All five CTS tests required

IDG-01, PDX-01, DBC-01, EIT-01, and ATR-01 must all pass regardless of tier.

Certification tiers

Choose your level

L1

Baseline

Self-attestation. Internal QA. Entry point for organizations demonstrating disclosure compliance with minimal operational overhead.

  • Signed self-attestation declaration
  • Internal QA documentation (script, logs)
  • All 5 CTS tests must pass
  • No external review required
  • Valid until major system change
L2

Operational

Production evidence reviewed. 30+ days of live call data demonstrating conformance. DFR must be below 2%.

  • 30+ days of production call records
  • DFR < 2% (Disclosure Failure Rate)
  • Evidence package review by NHID-Clinical
  • Audio sample review (optional but recommended)
  • Annual renewal
L3

Enterprise

Independent auditor. Continuous monitoring. Registry listing. Designed for large-scale deployments requiring the highest trust assurance.

  • Independent third-party auditor
  • Continuous monitoring evidence
  • Public registry listing (v1.4+)
  • Annual renewal with audit
  • L3 pilot contact required

How it works

Certification process

1

Request validation

Submit your system description and target tier through the validation request form. First validations are offered at no cost.

2

Receive CTS

We send the conformance test suite within 3 business days. You run the tests against your system and compile the evidence package.

3

Submit evidence

Submit your evidence package (call logs, transcripts, audio samples depending on tier). We review and confirm pass/fail on each test.

4

Receive badge

On confirmation, you receive your certification badge for use in product documentation, marketing, and payer agreements.

5

Maintain compliance

L1: re-certify on major system changes. L2/L3: annual renewal with updated evidence. L3 requires annual third-party audit.

→

Registry listing (v1.4+)

L3 certified organizations will be listed in the public NHID-Clinical registry for payer verification. Planned for v1.4+.
