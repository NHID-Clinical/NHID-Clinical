What problem does this solve?

Reduce impersonation latency before sensitive workflow data is exchanged.

NHID-Clinical gives payers, providers, and platforms a shared way to disclose automation, verify caller behavior, and escalate to a human when needed.

The green lane principle

A simple path for compliant AI voice workflows.

When AI agents identify themselves upfront and follow the rules, providers save time, payers reduce operational burden, and compliance teams gain auditable clarity.

01

Disclose before data exchange

02

Pass/fail conformance
