How to contribute

What we're looking for

💬

Technical feedback

Implementation feasibility, edge cases in the CTS tests, gaps in the conformance requirements, and interoperability concerns.

🏥

Real-world experience

Payer-side call handling patterns, AI caller behaviors you've encountered, and operational gaps the standard doesn't yet address.

⚖️

Compliance perspective

HIPAA officer feedback, payer operations input, and legal or regulatory analysis of the standard's positioning and scope claims.
