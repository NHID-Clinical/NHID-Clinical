L1 Minimum Evidence Checklist

Use this checklist to prepare your L1 validation submission. All four items are required (item 4 is strongly encouraged but optional during the pilot program). Submit completed evidence to validation@nhid-clinical.org after requesting validation at nhid-clinical.org/validation.html.

Done # Evidence Item
1 Transcript or call log with disclosure timestamp Sample call transcript or structured log showing non-human identity disclosure before first data request (NPI, member ID, claim number, or any identifier). Timestamp granularity to the second preferred. At least one representative sample required; 3–5 samples recommended.
2 Prompt or flow documentation (version-controlled) The agent's opening script or decision flow in a version-controlled record. A Git commit hash, internal version number, or document revision date is sufficient. Must show the disclosure is systematic — not incidental — and was in effect during the sampled calls.
3 Signed self-attestation declaration Completed attestation form (template provided after validation request) confirming your system meets all five CTS requirements (IDG-01 through ATR-01) as of submission date. Must be signed by an authorized representative of the submitting organization.
4 Audio sample — first 30 seconds Optional · Pilot Representative audio recording from call connect through first data solicitation. Optional during the pilot program. MP3, WAV, or secure shared link accepted. Strongly encouraged when transcript evidence alone is limited or ambiguous.
